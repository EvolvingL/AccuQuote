What is AccuQuote?

Quotes in minutes, not hours

AccuQuote lets you scan or photograph a room, answer a few quick questions about the job, and generates a full itemised quote — complete with materials list — in under 12 minutes. No spreadsheets. No guesswork.

12 min
Average quote time vs. 2.5 hours today
2
Your Tasks

What we're asking you to do

Over two weeks, complete five short tasks at your own pace. Each one takes 5–20 minutes.

Set Up Your Profile

Tell AccuQuote about your trade, your usual supplier, and the materials you order most.

Quote a Real Job

Use AccuQuote on an actual upcoming job — scan the room or enter dimensions manually.

Send the PDF

Generate and send the quote PDF to your customer, just as you would a normal quote.

Try a Custom Shape

Use the "Draw Room" feature to map an L-shaped or irregular space.

Complete the Exit Survey

A 5-minute form covering what worked, what didn't, and what you'd pay for it. Your single most important contribution to the product.
