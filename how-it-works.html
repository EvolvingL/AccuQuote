Questions

Plain answers

Do I need to be good with technology?

No. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use AccuQuote. It's designed for tradespeople who are on the tools, not sitting at a desk. No jargon, no complicated setup.

What trades does it work for?

Electricians, plumbers, plasterers, carpenters, decorators — anyone who needs to quote jobs and buy materials. You tell AccuQuote your trade and it adjusts to your way of working.

What if the room shape is unusual?

Draw it. AccuQuote has a custom room tool where you tap to add corners and walls — L-shapes, T-shapes, extensions, anything. It works out the area automatically.

How does it know the price of materials?

AccuQuote links up with trade supplier catalogues to pull current prices. You set your usual supplier once in your profile and it prices everything from there by default.

Can I change the quote before I send it?

Yes — you're always in control. AccuQuote builds the first draft, you review it, tweak anything you like, then send it. Your name, your numbers, your call.

Is there a contract or minimum term?

No contract. Pay monthly, cancel any time. If it's not saving you time, cancel — no questions asked.
