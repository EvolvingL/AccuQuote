Tell AccuQuote how you work

Answer a few plain-English questions: your trade, day rate, preferred supplier, material markup, and what you normally include in a quote. AccuQuote builds an AI profile from your answers — so every quote is priced exactly the way you'd price it yourself. You only do this once.

I'm a product manager. I've spent years building software products — working out what people actually need, stripping away the noise, and shipping things that work. There's no fluff here. No features designed to impress investors. Every decision in AccuQuote was made with a working tradesman sat next to us, telling us what would actually save them time on a Tuesday morning. We tested, we scrapped things, we rebuilt. The result is something that works the way tradespeople work — fast, reliable, no nonsense.

We didn't build AccuQuote to disrupt the trades. We built it because Dave the plasterer asked us to.

Trades quoting software is a tool that helps electricians, plasterers, builders, and other tradespeople build accurate job quotes faster than doing it manually. Traditional quoting involves measuring a room, calculating material quantities, looking up current supplier prices, writing up a document, and sending it — a process that can easily take two to three hours per job. Quoting software automates the measurement, material calculation, and document generation steps, so a tradesman can produce a professional quote in a fraction of the time. AccuQuote goes further by using LiDAR scanning and AI to generate a complete bill of materials from real supplier catalogues, making it the most complete job quoting app for UK tradespeople available today.

Third, AccuQuote formats everything into a clean, branded PDF quote that's ready to send directly from the app. The result is a complete, accurate quote — from scanning to sending — in under 20 minutes.
