How it works

Three steps from site visit to sent quote.

No training required. No templates to fill in. Just show up, do the survey, and send the quote before you leave the drive.

1

Measure the room

Walk the room with your phone. AccuQuote captures every dimension automatically — floor area, ceiling height, wall lengths, openings. Done in minutes, accurate to the centimetre.

2

Describe the job

Speak or type what needs doing — full rewire, re-plaster, new bathroom, loft conversion, whatever the job is. AccuQuote understands trade language and builds the scope automatically.

3

Send the quote

Get a full itemised quote with labour, materials, and live supplier prices from Screwfix, Toolstation, and CEF. Download as a branded PDF or share straight to WhatsApp — before you've left the drive.
