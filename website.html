How it works

Three steps. Quote sent before the kettle boils.

No training needed. No spreadsheet. If you can use WhatsApp, you can use AccuQuote.

1

Scan the space

Walk the room — or a whole house — with your phone. AccuQuote captures every dimension automatically: floor area, ceiling height, walls, openings. What took a tape measure and a notepad now takes seconds, accurate to the centimetre.

2

Describe the job

Speak or type what needs doing — "full rewire, 3-bed semi", "skim two bedrooms and the landing", whatever. AccuQuote understands trade language and works out every material, quantity and live supplier price. No catalogue. No product codes. No maths at 9pm.

3

Send the quote — and collect the deposit

A full itemised quote — labour, materials and live prices — sent before you leave the drive. The customer approves it in the app and gets a branded Stripe payment link straight away. They pay the deposit. You've got the job secured before you've reached the van.

Our story

It started with a decorator who knocked off at 2pm.

Our founder Luke runs Slick Digital, a development agency. When he started prospecting, he quickly noticed something: builders, carpenters, painters, electricians — they'd all quietly started using technology to work smarter. Not because anyone told them to. Because it was saving them real time.

Then he met Gary.

Gary is a decorator. Over a conversation that started with AI and ended somewhere around the third cup of tea, he walked Luke through his workflow — three separate apps cobbled together to scan spaces, work out materials, and get accurate quotes out fast. Live prices, proper numbers, professional PDFs.

That bit was impressive. This bit stopped Luke in his tracks: Gary was finishing work at 2pm. Every day.

He wasn't trying to grow an empire. He just wanted his afternoons back — time with his family, time to breathe, time that quoting used to steal every evening. Gary had found it. The process just happened to involve three apps that didn't quite talk to each other.

With Gary's help — and a group of master tradesmen who knew exactly where the pain was — Luke built AccuQuote: one app that does what Gary's three did, without the friction. Thousands of tradespeople are now getting real hours back every week. It's their time now, not the quote's.
